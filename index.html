About the Developer

Brittani Serna comes from Southern California and has began working as a web developer in 2021 after 7 years in the non-profit sector. She enjoys working with individuals, organizations, and enterprises of all sizes by assisting them in bringing their visions to life in the form of a landing page. She has experience as a full-stack developer but is particularly drawn to front end design to work to improve user experience.
